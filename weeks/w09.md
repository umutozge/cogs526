## Session 09


* PredLog refinements:
  * `Lions are carnivarious` vs. `Lions are in the building`.
  * `any`:
	* `Any one can join` vs. `Everyone can join`.
	* embed this into negative contexts.
  * relative clauses
  * Deixis vs. anaphor:
	- `If some student passes, I will be happy` vs. `If a student passes, s/he will be happy.`
  * `If anyone can do it, then anyone can do it.`
  * `only`
	* `Only men with a child can enter the park`
		- $\forall x. Ex \to Mx \land Cx$
		- $\forall x. Mx \land Ex \to Cx$
